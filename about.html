About Us

Who We Are

Roytech Inc. is an industrial automation and controls engineering company specializing in the automotive industry. Since 2018, we have delivered high-performance solutions across North America, supporting manufacturing operations throughout Canada, the United States, and Mexico.

What We Do

We provide end-to-end automation solutions including robotics integration, PLC programming, Ignition SCADA systems, MES and traceability, and IIoT architecture. Our work is focused on improving production efficiency, system visibility, and operational reliability in complex manufacturing environments.

Our Approach

Our team works closely with clients on-site to design, implement, and commission systems that meet the demands of modern automotive production. We prioritize reliability, scalability, and seamless integration—delivering solutions that perform in real-world conditions and high-volume environments.
